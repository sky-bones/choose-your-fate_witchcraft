About / Contact

Witchcraft is based on and inspired by the photographs of Rachel Baran, in particular these: Transience, The luckless ones, #Flickr12Days, and Touch the sky.

Witchcraft is a short choose-your-own adventure style story written by Elizabeth Nichols. I studied Graphic Design at Algonquin College, and graduated this spring. I love to tell stories and to design with a purpose.
